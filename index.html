⚖️ 2. Grammar: The Broken HR Memo

Upgraded Activity: We are reinforcing Parallel Structure! Read the grammar formula box carefully. Then, audit the 15 sentences from an HR Performance Review document and rewrite them so they have perfect grammatical symmetry.

🧮 The Parallel Structure Formula

To fix a broken sentence, identify the first item in the list. Force the other items to match its grammatical form.

Noun Formula:
List: Noun + Noun + Noun
"He loves coffee, tea, and water."
Gerund (-ing) Formula:
List: -ing Verb + -ing Verb + -ing Verb
"She enjoys reading, writing, and coding."
Infinitive Formula:
List: To + Verb + Verb + Verb
"We plan to meet, discuss, and decide."
Adjective Formula:
List: Adj + Adj + Adj
"The office is bright, clean, and quiet."

🕵️‍♂️ 4. Speaking: Advanced Consulting Cases

Activity: Your pair runs an elite consulting agency. Choose one complex client file below. Read the background and the constraints. Discuss a strategy using the Consultant Framework, and prepare a 2-minute pitch for the class. You must include one perfectly parallel list in your pitch!

1️⃣ Read & Solo Think (2m) ➔ 2️⃣ Group Strategy (4m) ➔ 3️⃣ The Class Pitch (2m)

Case A: The Burnout Tech Firm

Background: An app development company works 60-hour weeks. The office has video games, free food, and nap pods.
"I haven't seen my family in days. I don't want free pizza, I want to sleep."
The Goal: Propose 3 new self-management or scheduling rules to fix burnout without losing productivity.

Case B: The Chaotic Agency

Background: A marketing firm has a 100% open-plan office. No assigned desks, loud music, and dogs are allowed.
"I can't focus. Someone is always talking, and a dog ate my client report yesterday."
The Goal: Design a hybrid layout and propose 3 strict rules to restore focus and professionalism.

Case C: The Ghost Town

Background: A traditional law firm tried to allow remote work. Now, the huge, expensive office is completely empty.
"I come to the office and I'm alone all day. I don't even know my new colleagues."
The Goal: Create a compelling reason or schedule to bring people back to the office 2-3 days a week happily.

🧰 Consultant Toolkit (Mediation & Pitch Phrases)

"After analyzing the situation..." "We believe the root cause is..." "To solve this, we propose [Noun], [Noun], and [Noun]." "This compromise will help both sides."

✍️ 5. Writing: The Final Proposal

Activity: Write a formal/informal email to the CEO of the company you just analyzed in the Speaking section. Outline your 3 main recommendations. You must use parallel structure correctly.

📋 The CEO Proposal Blueprint
  • The Hook: Thank them for hiring your agency and state the purpose of the email.
  • The Diagnosis: Briefly state what the main problem is based on the employees' quotes.
  • The Solutions (Parallel): List your 3 recommendations using perfect parallel structure (e.g., "We recommend [verb-ing], [verb-ing], and [verb-ing].")
  • Sign-off: Offer a follow-up meeting and close professionally.

🗣️ Useful Proposal Phrases

The Hook: "Thank you for trusting our agency." "We are writing to propose..."
The Diagnosis: "Based on our analysis, the main issue is..."
The Solutions (Parallel!): "We recommend [verb-ing], [verb-ing], and [verb-ing]." "Our strategy involves [Noun], [Noun], and [Noun]."
Sign-off: "We would love to discuss this further."

🤖 AI Co-Thinker

Need inspiration? Review this sample proposal for Case A.

"Dear CEO,

Thank you for trusting our agency. After analyzing your office, it is clear that your team is suffering from severe burnout.

While the free food and games are nice, employees need rest. To fix this, we recommend limiting work weeks to 40 hours, removing the nap pods, and sending staff home at 5:00 PM. [Parallel -ing Verbs]

We believe this will make your team healthier, happier, and more productive.

Best regards,
The Consultants"
